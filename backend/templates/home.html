Your Cards

To aid the speed and ease in memorizing anything, making as many unique connections in your mind is essential. Especially wiht a language like Chinese, where one can encounter hundreds of distinct characters in a single conversation that must me understood to accurately respond, being able to retrieve the meaning of the character from its appearance or sounds is paramount to becoming a conversational speaker. This tool helps to provide ideas for locking characters in memory using quick and easy association suggestions.
